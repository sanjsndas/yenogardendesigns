About Yeno Garden Designs

At Yeno Garden Designs, we specialize in creating custom garden landscapes that blend elegance with nature’s tranquility. Our team of expert garden designers brings years of experience crafting outdoor spaces that embody the spirit of the forest — lush greenery, natural textures, and serene atmospheres.

Whether you envision a woodland retreat, a stylish terrace garden, or a vibrant flower haven, we focus on sustainable, eco-friendly practices combined with expert horticultural knowledge. Each design is tailored to the unique layout, soil, and microclimate of your property, ensuring long-lasting beauty and enjoyment.

Our passion is bringing outdoor dreams to life, crafting serene sanctuaries where every tree, shrub, and blossom tells a story.

Our Garden Design Services

Woodland Garden Design

Inspired by the beauty of natural forests, we create woodland-inspired gardens with native trees, ferns, and wildflowers to give your space a peaceful, shaded retreat feel amidst urban settings.

Terrace and Patio Garden

Designing elegant terraces with integrated planters, seating areas, and greenery to create perfect outdoor living spaces that blend comfort with natural elements.

Flower Garden & Seasonal Planting

Expert planning and planting of flower beds, combining seasonal blooms and perennial plants to guarantee year-round color, fragrance, and charm.

Sustainable & Eco Garden Planning

Implementing eco-conscious design approaches including rainwater harvesting, native plants, composting solutions, and wildlife habitats.

Custom Garden Lighting

Designing subtle outdoor lighting schemes to highlight your garden’s best features and create inviting ambiance during dusk and night hours.

Garden Consultation & Planning

One-on-one expert consultations to understand your needs, site analysis, and detailed garden planning for do-it-yourself gardeners or landscapers.
