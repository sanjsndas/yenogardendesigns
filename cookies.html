Cookies Policy for Yeno Garden Designs

This comprehensive Cookies Policy explains how Yeno Garden Designs uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your experience, analyze use, and comply with legal requirements.

Introduction to Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit them. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

Understanding cookies, how we use them, and your rights helps you make informed decisions when browsing our website and using our services.

This policy covers the types of cookies we use, their purposes, how to control them, and related privacy implications in detail.

We encourage you to read this policy carefully and reach out with any questions using our contact info found at the bottom of this page.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies on our website, each serving different functions. These include:

1. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary to enable basic functions like page navigation, secure access to parts of our site, and facilitating your interaction with our services. Without these cookies, the website may not perform optimally.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, which pages are most popular, and performance issues. This information helps us improve our website’s usability and functionality.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices you make such as your preferred language, font size, or location to provide a more personalized experience.

4. Targeting or Advertising Cookies

While we do not directly use these, third parties may place cookies to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing activity across other websites. These cookies track your browsing habits and may share information with advertisers.

5. Social Media Cookies

Our website contains social media features that may place cookies to enable you to share content or interact with social platforms.

How We Use Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions securely and efficiently.
  • Remember your preferences and settings for future visits.
  • Analyze user behavior and traffic to improve content and layout.
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Deliver tailored advertisements by third-party partners, if you opt-in.

Our cookie usage helps provide a smooth browsing experience and supports our business goals in a transparent way.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You have the choice to accept or reject cookies. Many browsers allow you to control cookie preferences through settings:

  • You can block or delete cookies using your browser settings, extensions, or privacy modes.
  • Most browsers accept cookies by default but offer features to clear or limit cookie usage.
  • Disabling cookies may impact website functionality, user experience, and some services.

We provide a cookie consent dialog on our website to give you control over which categories of cookies you authorize. You can update your preferences anytime by revisiting the cookie dialog or using browser controls.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party service providers such as analytics companies, advertising networks, and social media platforms may deploy cookies on our website.

The placement, use, and management of such cookies fall under their respective privacy policies and practices.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third parties to understand their cookie usage and options.

Legal Framework and Compliance

We comply with data protection laws including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) with respect to cookies.

This means:

  • You must be informed about cookies used and their purposes.
  • You have the right to consent or object to cookie usage.
  • We maintain records of your consent and allow you to withdraw it.
  • We only place non-essential cookies with your explicit consent.

Impact of Cookies on Your Privacy

Cookies can collect identifiers and browsing behavior, which can be personal data under privacy laws.

We take your privacy seriously and ensure:

  • Your data collected via cookies is handled securely.
  • We do not sell your personal data obtained from cookies.
  • You have full control to accept or reject cookies through clear choices.

Understanding cookie functions helps you better control your online privacy and experience.

Opting Out and Cookie Preferences

You can opt out of certain cookie categories by:

  • Declining cookies in the consent dialog on our website.
  • Using browser settings to disable third-party cookies or all cookies.
  • Installing browser extensions that block trackers and cookies.
  • Opting out of targeted advertising via industry opt-out platforms.

Please note that opting out of some cookies may impair some website functions and services we provide.

Data Retention Related to Cookies

Cookies and tracking technologies may store data for varied durations:

  • Session cookies last only during your visit and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies remain on your device for specified periods to remember preferences or track usage over time.

We do not retain personal data beyond what is necessary for the purposes described or required by law.

Cookies and Minors

Our website is not intended for children under 16 years old.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors or use cookies targeted specifically at children.
