Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does a garden design project typically take?
    Project timelines vary depending on scale and complexity. Generally, initial consultations take 1-2 weeks, detailed design plans 3-6 weeks, followed by phased implementation based on client preferences.
  • Can you work with existing landscapes?
    Yes, we specialize in enhancing existing gardens, integrating new features, improving plant health, and optimizing layouts while respecting your current environment and preferences.
  • What areas do you serve?
    We primarily serve the New York City metropolitan area and surrounding regions. Please contact us for availability in other locations.
  • Do you offer maintenance services?
    While we focus mainly on design, we partner with trusted local landscapers who provide professional maintenance services. We can recommend and coordinate these services for you.
  • How can I schedule a consultation?
    You can contact us via phone, email, or the contact form on this page. We’ll schedule a convenient time to discuss your garden goals and options.
  • Are your garden designs eco-friendly?
    Absolutely. Sustainability is core to our philosophy. We emphasize native plants, water conservation, and habitat creation in all our designs.
  • What are your payment terms?
    Payment terms will be clearly outlined in your service contract. We generally accept deposits upfront with progress payments as work proceeds.
  • Can I request custom features?
    Yes, our team loves custom challenges. We work closely to tailor designs that match your vision, lifestyle, and budget.
  • Do you provide garden lighting design?
    Yes, lighting is an important element. We offer custom lighting plans that enhance beauty and safety but remain discreet and eco-conscious.
  • How do I give feedback?
    Feedback is welcome via email or phone anytime. We value your input to improve services and client satisfaction.
